Sidereal Astrology:

The Tropical zodiac is the system used by most Western astrologers. It's based on the Earth's seasons and divides the sky into twelve equal 30-degree signs. Tropical astrology describes how cosmic energies express themselves through your personality and daily life. The Sidereal zodiac, on the other hand, aligns with the actual constellations in the sky. Each sign covers a different portion of the ecliptic, revealing deeper insights into your soul's karmic path and spiritual evolution. By exploring both systems side by side, you gain a more complete and nuanced understanding of yourself—connecting the outer personality with the inner essence.
