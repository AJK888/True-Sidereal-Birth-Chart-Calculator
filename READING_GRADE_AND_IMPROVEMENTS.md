## üí° What Makes a Great Reading

A great astrological reading should:

1. **Show the Evidence Chain**
   - Every claim should trace back to specific chart factors
   - Show HOW the chart creates the pattern
   - Explain the mechanism, not just the result

2. **Provide Behavioral Specificity**
   - Not "you tend to be emotional"
   - But "when criticized, you [specific action] because [chart factor]"

3. **Explain the "Why"**
   - Not just "you have this pattern"
   - But "you have this pattern because [chart factor] creates [need/fear], which shows up as [behavior]"

4. **Show Connections**
   - How do themes interact?
   - How do aspects modify each other?
   - What's the hierarchy of influences?

5. **Be Comprehensive**
   - Cover all major chart factors
   - Don't skip houses or aspects
   - Show the full picture
